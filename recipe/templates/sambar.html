Sambar

Info

Time
1-2 Hours
Medium
Servings
1

Ingredients

1 large onion, chopped
1 cup okra, chopped
1/2 cup drumstick pieces
1/4 cup pumpkin, cubed
1/4 cup brinjal, cubed
1/4 cup carrots, chopped
1 tablespoon sambar powder
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
1 cup toor dal
1 tamarind ball
1 teaspoon jaggery
Salt to taste
Ghee or vegetable oil
1 cup water

Directions

  1. Wash and soak toor dal in water for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a pressure cooker. Add the sambar masala and cook for a few seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add the soaked toor dal, tamarind ball, jaggery, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add 1 cup water and pressure cook for 3-4 whistles.
  6. Once the pressure is released, add the chopped vegetables and cook until tender.
  7. Adjust the consistency of the sambar by adding more water if needed.
  8. Serve hot with rice or idli.
