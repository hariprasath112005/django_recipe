Chicken Biriyani

Info

Time
1-2 Hours
Medium
Servings
1

Ingredients

1 kg basmati rice
1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 large onion, finely chopped
1-inch piece ginger, minced
2 bay leaves
1 cinnamon stick
1 cardamom pod
1 teaspoon cumin powder
1 teaspoon coriander powder
1 teaspoon garam masala
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/4 teaspoon red chili powder
Salt to taste
Ghee or vegetable oil
1 cup yogurt
1/2 cup green coriander, chopped

Directions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a bowl, combine chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger, garlic, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a large pot. Add bay leaves, cinnamon stick, cloves, and cardamom pod. Fry until fragrant.
  4. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the chopped onions and cook until golden brown.
  6. Stir in the remaining spices (cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and red chili powder). Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add the soaked rice, water (about 1.5 times the volume of rice), and salt. Bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid has been absorbed.
  9. Let the biryani rest for 5-10 minutes before serving.
  10. Garnish with chopped green coriander.
