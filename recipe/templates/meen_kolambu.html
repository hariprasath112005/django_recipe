Meen kolambu

Info

Time
30-45 Mins
Medium
Servings
1

Ingredients

1 kg fish, cut into pieces (e.g., seer fish, salmon)
Salt to taste
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1 tablespoon sambar powder
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 cup tamarind pulp
1/4 cup coconut milk
1/4 teaspoon jaggery
Ghee or vegetable oil
3 cups water

Directions

  1. Wash and pat dry the fish pieces. Marinate with salt and turmeric powder for 15-20 minutes.
  2. Heat ghee or vegetable oil in a saucepan.
  3. Add the kolambu masala and cook for a few seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the chopped onion and cook until golden brown.
  5. Add the marinated fish pieces and cook until browned on all sides.
  6. Add the tamarind pulp, coconut milk, jaggery, and salt. Stir well.
  7. Add 3 cups water and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until the fish is cooked and the gravy has thickened.
  9. Serve hot with rice or idli.
