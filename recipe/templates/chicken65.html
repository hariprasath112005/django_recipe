Chicken 65

Info

Time
20-30 Mins
Medium
Servings
1

Ingredients

1 kg chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 cup yogurt
1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
1 teaspoon chili powder
1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
1/2 teaspoon garam masala
1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
Salt to taste
Oil for frying

Directions

  1. In a bowl, combine chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium-high heat. Drain the marinated chicken from the yogurt and add it to the hot oil. Fry until golden brown and crispy.
  3. In the same pan, add a few tablespoons of the oil used for frying. Add 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and cook for a few seconds. Add 1/2 cup water, 1 tablespoon chili powder, 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder, and salt to taste. Bring to a boil and simmer for a few minutes until the sauce thickens.
  4. Return the fried chicken to the sauce and toss to coat. Cook for a few minutes more to let the flavors meld.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.
